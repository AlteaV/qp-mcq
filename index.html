🛠️ For Schools (K-12, IIT-JEE & NEET Prep)

Master the Competitive Edge.
From School Exams to National Ranks.

Streamline your weekly test schedules, internal assessments, and mock examinations. TestNinja handles complex formatting for advanced science and math, making it the perfect engine for demanding competitive prep.

National Exam Ready: Out-of-the-box templates mirroring exact exam patterns for IIT-JEE (Advanced/Mains) and NEET, alongside standard Grade 6–12 board curriculums.
Smart Question Banks: Upload your existing content via PDFs or images. Our AI categorizes questions by difficulty, subject matter, and cognitive level (Bloom’s Taxonomy).
Classroom Diagnostic Reports: Teachers instantly see which concepts (e.g., Organic Chemistry Mechanisms or Quadratic Equations) the entire cohort struggled with, allowing for immediate lesson adjustment.
🎓 For Higher Education & Universities

Maximize Employability with
Data-Driven Campus Placements.

Transition your computer labs and examination halls into a scalable, secure digital hub. Prepare your students to clear rigorous corporate recruitment filters.

Placement MCQ Modules: Thousands of pre-vetted, industry-aligned MCQs covering Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, and Core Engineering concepts used by top-tier tech and consulting giants.
Neo-Inspired Secure Exams: Cloud-based, high-concurrency engine that handles thousands of students testing simultaneously with zero latency.
